About this website



This site was created by Naoki Takahashi of

Ryugasaki Daiichi High School in Ibaraki Prefecture

for the "全国情報教育" contest.


In addition, the creation is made possible

with the support of foreign language supporters.



The purpose of creating this site


I wanted to put together an ideal solution that would solve the contemporary issues

that I was interested in and give it some kind of shape.

Through entering this contest, I wanted to create an opportunity

for as many people as possible to know about it.



Notes


All of the shops, events, etc. listed on this site are ideal or imaginary and do not actually exist.

Please note that the author will not be held responsible in any way for the use of this site.

After all, this site is made by an amateur high school student.

I would be happy if you could see it with forgiving eyes.
